✦ HOW IT WORKS

From Your Idea to
Your Doorstep

No design skills needed. Just describe what you want — our AI handles the rest. Here's how it works in four simple steps.

01
Illustration coming soon
Step 1

Share Your Idea

Type anything that comes to mind. "A wolf howling at a neon moon." "My dog Max as a superhero." "Something vintage and bold for our bowling team." There are no wrong answers — the wilder the better.

💡 Pro tip: You can also upload a reference photo — a pet, a logo, a sketch — and our AI will use it as inspiration.

02
Illustration coming soon
Step 2

Answer 2–3 Smart Questions

Our AI asks a couple of quick follow-up questions to nail the details — colors, mood, style, placement. Takes about 30 seconds. The more specific you are, the better your design turns out.

💡 Pro tip: You can also choose Front Only or Front & Back before generating — great for teams who want something on both sides.

03
Illustration coming soon
Step 3

Perfect Your Design

Your design appears on a shirt preview instantly. Don't love it? Request changes — as many times as you need. Tweak the colors, the style, the composition. You only move forward when it's exactly right.

💡 Pro tip: After your design generates, use the Switch Style bar to instantly try the same concept in Graphic, Cartoon, Realistic, Painting, Hyperrealistic, or Artistic — no regenerating needed.

04
Illustration coming soon
Step 4

Order & We Handle the Rest

Pick your size, shirt color, and quantity. Add multiple colors to your order if you want the same design on different shirts. We print everything in-house and ship straight to your door in 5–7 business days.

💡 Pro tip: The design is always free — you only pay when you place your order. Save designs to come back to them later.

Ready to Make Your Shirt?

It's free to design. Takes about 2 minutes. No account required to start.
